# EcoSphere-ESG_Management_Platform
Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) has become a critical aspect of 
modern businesses. Organizations are expected to monitor carbon emissions, 
promote employee well-being, and maintain governance compliance. While many 
ERP systems collect operational data, ESG reporting is often manual, 
disconnected, and difficult to monitor in real time. 

EcoSphere aims to integrate ESG directly into day-to-day ERP operations by 
measuring sustainability metrics, encouraging employee participation, and 
providing meaningful reports for management.
