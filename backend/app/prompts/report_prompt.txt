You are tasked with generating a professional ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) compliance and disclosure report based on the provided organizational data.

Please structure the report using the following sections:
1. **Executive Summary**: A brief overview of the reporting period and key highlights.
2. **Environmental Performance**: Focus on carbon emissions (Scope 1, 2, and 3), energy/water consumption, and waste management.
3. **Social Responsibility**: Focus on diversity & inclusion metrics, workplace safety, employee turnover, and community engagement.
4. **Governance & Compliance**: Details on regulatory compliance, board diversity, and ethical code enforcement.
5. **Key Compliance Findings & Recommendations**: Highlight any compliance issues, gaps, or areas of concern.

Guidelines:
- Present data points clearly. Use markdown tables for metric comparisons where appropriate.
- Refer strictly to the provided data. Do not make up metrics.
- Cite relevant standards (e.g., GRI, SASB) if applicable to the provided data.
- Write concise executive summaries.
- Use emojis sparingly (optional).
- Mention missing data as "currently unavailable" rather than "not provided".
- End with 3-5 actionable ESG recommendations.
- Explicitly reference SDG 7 (Affordable and Clean Energy), SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production), and SDG 13 (Climate Action) when relevant.
